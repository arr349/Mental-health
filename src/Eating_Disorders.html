Eating Disorders

Eating disorders are very common, especially among the younger generation. There could be many reasons for eating disorders. On this page you can find some resources and information about these disorders. Here you can watch a video about Anorexia Nervosa, Bulimia, and Binge Eating Disorder.

There are a lot of eating disorders. In this section, we will review only some of them. You will find a lot of links to the resources where you can find out more.

List of Eating Disorders

  • Anorexia nervosa
  • Bulimia nervosa
  • Binge eating disorder
  • Pica
  • Rumination disorder
  • Avoidant/restrictive food intake disorder

If you want to find out more about any of the disorders listed above, you can visit a following website.

There are multiple reasons for different eating disorders. It can be genetics, imbalance in hormones, nutritional deficiencies. It also can include low self-esteem and negative body image. Please visit this website to find out more.

Treatment

  • Healthy eating
  • Psychotherapy
  • Hospitalization

For more information visit Mayo Clinic website
