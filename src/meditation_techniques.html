Meditation Techniques

Overview

There are numerous different types of meditation. There is "basic" meditation, focused meditation, activity-orientated meditation, mindfulness meditation, and spiritual meditation.

Researchers generally classify meditation into two categories: concentrative and non-concentrative. We will only look at two.

Concentrative Techniques

These techniques involve focusing on a particular object surrounding someone. This object can be a candle, mantra, or even wall. This technique is very similar to grounding.

Non-Concentrative Techniques

These techniques involve focusing on senses inside someone. These "senses" could be the sounds in the environment, your own breathing, the feeling you have in a certain part of your body, etc.
