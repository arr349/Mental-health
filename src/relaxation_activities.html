Relaxation Activities

  • Listen to relaxing music - Listening to the right kind of music can help calm you down and destress.
  • Meditate - Meditation can help you become more mindful along with being a healthy relaxation activity. There are numerous benefits to meditation.
  • Spend time with a pet - Spending time with a pet can be very relaxing. Research has backed this up as well.
  • Pray - If you are religious, saying a repetitive prayer can help clear up your mind and provide focus. Even if you're not religious, repeating a positive affirmation instead of a prayer can work too.
